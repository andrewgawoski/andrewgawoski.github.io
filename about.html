About

Hey there! Thanks for coming to the part of my portfolio with information about me as a person. If I was nearby, I'd be pretty excited that you're interested in knowing a little bit more about my life. I'm a 24-year old programmer, San Francisco Bay Area native, and University of California Santa Cruz alumnus.

I studied computer science with a focus in game design when I was down at Santa Cruz, that particular department being my reason for applying to begin with. I currently spend a good bit of time writing code, trying to solve design problems, and playing a bunch of different games. But if I'm not, I'm probably playing tennis, watching baseball, drinking tea or otherwise "wasting" time by having fun.

I had mild exposure to web design over a decade ago in 2005 and I didn't start working on games until 2007. I first got my feet wet in 2010 when starting programming in C++. While working towards graduation, I contributed towards a good handful of group projects, with a few of them released on Kongregate. There are definitely some I'm not satisfied with and prefer don't see the light of day, but I'd rather look forward to what's next.

Since graduation in 2016, I've worked on personal projects like Luna Limit and more recently an action platformer I'm calling Perdix. But, as fantastic as working on solo projects is, I don't think it gets much better than sharing fantastic ideas and trying to find ways of making them work. I can be a veritable social butterfly and the thought have having greater resources with a team-based environment delights me.
