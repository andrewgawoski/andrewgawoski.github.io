About Andrew Gawoski

You want confidence?

I’ve developed and prototyped games independently as Umbrellabell Software since graduating UCSC with a B.Sc. In Computer Science: Computer Game Design. My focus has gradually shifted towards design from my more rigorous technical background as was needed for those projects. The work I’ve done outside of design or development has only sharpened my knowledge and appreciation for the difficulties of other disciplines involved in game development and their workflows. I pride myself on my research and analysis skills, as well as how quickly I can learn new development techniques.

Currently, I’m developing a farming life simulation title, Dawnflower Labyrinth, that will blow the genre away. Exceptionally delightful and delightfully exceptional, the game is still incredibly early in development. A large portion of the design-work has been documented. Asset creation is progressing as quickly as a designer/programmer wearing the hat of a technical artist/artist can make new release-ready meshes, materials, and textures.
