About Andrew Gawoski

You want confidence?

I’ve developed and prototyped games independently since graduating from UCSC with a B.Sc. In Computer Science: Computer Game Design. My focus has gradually shifted towards design as I've built systems and worlds from scratch. All of the work I’ve done outside of design or development has only helped me appreciate the many disciplines involved in game development. I pride myself on my research and analysis skills, as well as how quickly I can learn new development techniques. I can learn to do anything and do it well.

Currently, I’m developing a farming life simulation title, Dawnflower Labyrinth, that will blow the genre away. I'm not making it because I think it's good, I'm making it because I hope it will be great. A large portion of the design-work has been completed and work is coming along smoothly, or as smoothly as a designer making textures, materials, and meshes can manage.
