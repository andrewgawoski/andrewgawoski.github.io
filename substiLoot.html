SubstiLoot (2015)

About the Project

SubstiLoot is a single player cooperative text-based puzzle game created with Inform 7, a design system for interactive fiction. Players guide hopeful treasure-seekers Percy and Bors, loosely inspired by Percival and Bors of Arthurian legend, to wonderous hidden riches.

Features

  • Play as a handsome treasure hunter!
  • Play as the other, far better looking, treasure hunter!
  • Solve door puzzles
  • Explore treasure-filled ruins

Play Now!

When developing SubstiLoot as a pair as coursework in early 2015, most of the time spent working on the project was spent trying to make mechanically complicated features in an engine for interactive fiction and web-based narrative. As a relatively niche platform, documentation seemed scarce for much more than basic multiple choice narratives despite having several examples of interesting and complex experiences. Several features were easy to accomplish in our small, locked-door puzzle-based adventure. Locking doors with keys, or otherwise requiring items to proceed somewhere was easy to do. Creating a room layout and properly linking them together, including an intentionally illogical layout worked without a hitch.

At the time, we took on the challenge of implementing a system of weighted pressure plates that players would need to stand on top of. There were a number of syntax issues related to the plates themselves, unexpected user input, not the least of which was picking up pressure plates or the existing on top of state in Inform7. But with pressure plate puzzles comes the need for weights, and we also attempted to allow users to control multiple characters in the adventure.
