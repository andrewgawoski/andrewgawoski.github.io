A Squire to Greatness

 An exploration-focused RPG with card battle elements where a squire learns under the protection of a knight from the neighboring kingdom and then mentors the next generation. Gameplay focuses on multiple layers or player progression and deck-building including a generational or inheritance-styled system.

 A goal I've had in developing A Squire to Greatness is to create an environment where players can make interesting choices with consequences that span multiple character generations. I've been working independently on development in Unity and am excited to continue onwards towards the game's release.

Luna Limit

 A single player tile-based game with a strong math influence. Players aim to create as many full moons as possible by flipping tiles and adding moon phases.

SubstiLoot

 Developed as a pair for the Interactive Narrative course at UCSC using Inform 7. Players control two characters by changing perspectives between the two (swap) in order to explore a puzzle and treasure filled ruin.
