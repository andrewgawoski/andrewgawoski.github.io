Dawnflower Labyrinth

Dawnflower Labyrinth is a farming life-simulation RPG similar to the Bokujō Monogatari series and developed in UE4.18 with cooperative play in mind. With fairly well established genre conventions, players raise animals, grow fruits and vegetables, and interact with NPC townsfolk including a handful of potential romantic partners. The game began development in early 2018, and will continue until it can stand alongside the games that influenced it.
