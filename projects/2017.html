Ascengine

Prototype developed independently in 2017 as a refresher for UE4 development. Ascengine was designed as a zero-gravity dual-stick racer utilizing standard console controller inputs where players control two engines separately. The game aimed to be heavily physics-based and accurate to concepts such as torgue and acceleration while completely ignoring physics to keep vehicles attached to racetracks. Development halted when the defining system was found to be more interesting than fun.

PCG Cave

Prototype for Harvest Moon-styled mining floor generation developed in Unity. Included groundwork for community-sourced localization, inventory, item-drops/loot-tables, stamina and upgrade systems as it was at some point considered for fleshed-out development as a game. Many of the concepts and ideas used here, although not particularly groundbreaking, are similar to those in Dawnflower Labyrinth (current project) as they come from the same root genre inspiration.

Markdown Parser

Markdown to HTML converter project I wrote based off language standards from Daringfireball and features of their Dingus converter. Replicates a number of formatting quirks from their application and is written in C#. Provided mostly as a more complete example of written code.
