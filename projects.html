Games & other projects

Luna Limit

2016

Luna Limit is a single player tile-based game with a strong simple math influence. Players activate tiles to change surrounding moon phases in a way similar to adding simple numbers. Tasked with a single objective, players attempt to create as many full moons as possible.

Summoner Timers

2016

Summoner Timers is an assistant application for players of Riot Games’ League of Legends. The application allows players to keep track of the cooldown for various different in game abilities not displayed by the game’s UI. Users may select from automatic and manual ability entry.

Say La Vie

2015

Help best friends Steve and Sven translate the recipe for space-gluten free croissants by retrieving the lost language of French in Say La Vie, an educational language-learning game. As their time travelling robot, Croissant, explore a small town in northern France, interact with villagers, and complete simple tasks to fill your dictionary and return to the future.

SubstiLoot

2015

SubstiLoot is a single player cooperative text-based puzzle game created with Inform 7, a design system for interactive fiction. Players guide hopeful treasure-seekers Percy and Bors, loosely inspired by Percival and Bors of Arthurian legend, to wonderous hidden riches.
