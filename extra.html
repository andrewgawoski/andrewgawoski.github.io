Extra Things!

One of the biggest problems I run into frequently, and while coding, is overthinking things. Sometimes I'll inexplicably avoid a simple solution because, "It can't be that simple, right?"

I can cook well enough, fish, drive a manual transmission and read sheet music. I'm secretly proud of my handwriting. I prefer tea and usually won't drink coffee.

I'll infrequently play card games like Dominion and Magic the Gathering, and enjoy the deck-building aspect of working with limited resources.

I'm excited about Perdix because it plays to my love of classical mechanics and because I'm able to include aspects from cultures around the world that I've studied as a student.
